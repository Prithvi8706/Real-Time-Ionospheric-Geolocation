RTIL‑GEO
Real-Time Ionospheric Geolocation
DRDO  DIA‑CoE/EW/02  ·  Single-Station HF Emitter Localisation
OPERATIONAL
This system estimates the location of a high-frequency (HF) radio transmitter from a single receiving station using the Single-Station Location (SSL) method. HF signals “skip” off the ionosphere — a charged layer of the upper atmosphere. By measuring the signal’s arrival bearing and elevation and modelling the ionospheric height, the pipeline back-calculates the transmitter’s ground position. Enter your station coordinates, the measured signal angles, and current space-weather conditions, then click Compute.
